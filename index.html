About

I am an ESA Research Fellow in Space Science at ESTEC in Noordwijk, Netherlands.

Before joining ESA in 2023, I completed a PhD in Solar Physics at Mullard Space Science Laboratory, University College London.

My work uses spectroscopy and coordinated observations to study coronal plasma composition, solar flares, and related solar activity.

Science interests

  • Coronal plasma composition and first ionisation potential bias.
  • Spectroscopic signatures of flare onset and flare evolution.
  • Coordinated observations across Solar Orbiter, Hinode, IRIS, JVLA, GREGOR, and SST.
  • Public datasets and comparative analysis of Hinode/EIS flare spectroscopy.
